CATCH THE FLAME

2:00 PM

The Olympic Flame has escaped from the top of the mountain. And he's a very good snowboarder. Catch him to win medals.

Athletes per nation: 1

Venue: Tuckered Out Trail (blue)

Judge: Harry Bugden

Rules: Athletes will line up at the top of the Tuckered Out Trail. The Olympic Flame will be giving a head-start. Only when the M.O.I.S.T. judge fires the starting gun will athletes begin chase. The winner is the athlete who pulls free the gold flag from around the flame's waist. Silver and Bronze will be awarded to the athletes that pull the silver flag or bronze flag from around the flame's waist
