Code of Conduct

As well as representing a nation, all athletes are representing the company. Here's how to avoid making us look bad.

  • Don't break anything.
  • Don't go anywhere you shouldn't go.
  • Don't take anything or leave anything.
  • Don't drink more than your Mother would.
  • Don't do anything you wouldn't do in the office.
  • Don't even think about banned substances.
  • Remember to say 'please' and 'thank you'.
